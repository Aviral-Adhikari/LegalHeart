About Legal Heart

Our Story

Founded by Senior Advocate Krishna Prashad Subedi and Advocate Arjun Adhikari, Legal Heart has grown from a small practice to one of Nepal's most respected law firms. Our journey is marked by a commitment to excellence, integrity, and client success.

We believe in the power of justice and the importance of accessible legal services. Every case we handle is approached with the same dedication and attention to detail, regardless of its size or complexity.
