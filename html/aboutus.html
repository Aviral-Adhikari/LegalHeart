About Us

With Heart, For Justice. Your Trusted Legal Partner in Nepal.

Legal Heart Pvt. Ltd.

Legal Heart Pvt. Ltd. is a distinguished law firm based in Nepal, built on a foundation of trust, experience, and an unwavering commitment to justice. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, high-quality legal services that meet the evolving needs of our clients—whether individuals, businesses, or institutions.

Senior Advocate Krishna Prashad Subedi
Advocate Arjun Adhikari

The firm was established by two of Nepal's most experienced and respected legal professionals—Senior Advocate Krishna Prashad Subedi and Advocate Arjun Adhikari. With decades of practice in the legal field, both founders bring extensive expertise in a broad range of areas including civil and criminal litigation, constitutional law, corporate advisory, family disputes, and alternative dispute resolution. Their leadership, insight, and ethical standards have helped shape Legal Heart into a firm known for its reliability, strategic thinking, and results-driven approach.

At Legal Heart, we understand that every case is more than just a legal file—it represents real people, real stakes, and real challenges. That's why we take a personalized approach to each matter, ensuring careful attention to detail, clear communication, and a deep understanding of our clients' goals.
